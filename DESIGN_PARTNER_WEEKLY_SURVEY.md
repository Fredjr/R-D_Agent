## Analysis Framework

### Weekly Analysis (30 minutes):
1. **Usage Metrics**: How many people used it? How often?
2. **Feature Adoption**: Which features are most/least used?
3. **Sentiment**: What's the overall sentiment (positive/negative)?
4. **Bugs**: What bugs were reported? Priority?
5. **Feature Requests**: What features are requested? By how many?
6. **NPS**: What's the average NPS score?

### Monthly Analysis (2 hours):
1. **Trends**: How is usage changing over time?
2. **Retention**: Are people using it consistently?
3. **Top Issues**: What are the recurring complaints?
4. **Top Requests**: What features are most requested?
5. **Success Stories**: What positive feedback stands out?
